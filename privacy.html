Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2026

Draft, not yet legally reviewed. This describes how Echo's data flows actually work today, verified directly against the code rather than guessed. It has not been reviewed by a lawyer — have counsel confirm it meets GDPR's requirements (legal basis, retention periods, your supervisory authority) before relying on it for real users.

1. Who controls your data

Bright Peak GmbH, Kürnbergstraße 32a, 81369 München, Germany (HRB 286591), is the data controller for the Service. Contact us at contact@echotxt.ai for any privacy request.

2. What we collect, and why

Account data. Your email address, used to sign you in (a one-time code, no password) and to identify your subscription. Stored in our database (hosted on Supabase) for as long as your account exists.

Message content, when you use Echo. When you press a hotkey, Echo may capture a screenshot of the active application window (Modes A/B) or just the text you type (Mode C — templates). This is sent to our backend, which forwards it to Anthropic's Claude API to draft a reply, then returns the draft to you. We do not store the screenshot or the message content — it is processed to generate your draft and then discarded on our side. Anthropic processes it under their own API terms as our AI sub-processor; we do not control how long Anthropic itself retains API request data, and recommend checking Anthropic's commercial terms directly for their current policy.

Usage metadata. We log how many replies you draft each month, which mode and AI model were used, and token counts — needed to enforce plan limits and to understand our own AI costs. This does not include the message content itself, only counts.

Personal context you configure. Tone-of-voice profiles, templates, signatures, and any personal/contact details you enter in Settings are stored locally on your own device, not on our servers. They're only transmitted to our backend at the moment you draft a reply, as part of building that reply's prompt.

Billing data. If you subscribe to Premium, Stripe (our payment processor) collects your payment details directly — we never see or store your full card number. We store your Stripe customer/subscription IDs and subscription status to know whether you have Premium access.

3. Who we share data with

  • Anthropic (Claude API) — processes message content to draft replies, as described above.
  • Supabase — hosts our database, authentication, and backend functions (sub-processor for all account/usage/billing data above).
  • Stripe — processes payments and manages subscriptions.

We don't sell your data, and we don't share it with anyone else.

4. Where your data is processed

Our sub-processors may process data outside the EU/EEA (including the United States). Where that happens, we rely on the safeguards those providers offer for international transfers (such as the EU Standard Contractual Clauses). 5. How long we keep data

Account and usage data: for as long as your account is active, plus a reasonable period afterward for billing and legal-compliance records. Message content itself: not retained by us at all, as described above. You can request deletion of your account at any time (see below).

6. Your rights

If you're in the EU/UK, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you;
  • Correct inaccurate data;
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data;
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format;
  • Object to or restrict certain processing;
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection supervisory authority.

To exercise any of these, email contact@echotxt.ai.

7. Children

Echo is not directed at children, and we don't knowingly collect data from anyone under 16.

8. Changes to this policy

We'll update this page if how we handle data changes, and note the date at the top.
