Why Echo

Built to get out of your way.

No copy-pasting into a chatbot, no switching windows, no app-specific integration to set up. Echo works with whatever's already open.

Reads any app

Email, chat, SMS — Echo captures whatever's on screen and understands it with Claude's vision, no per-app integration required.

Sounds like you

Set up tone-of-voice profiles once. Echo drafts replies that match how you actually write, not a generic AI voice.

You always hit send

Echo pastes a draft into the focused field. It never sends anything on its own — sending is always your manual, deliberate step.

Saved templates

For the replies you send all the time — pick a template, add a few details, paste. No screenshot or AI call needed.

Real email signatures

HTML signatures with your logo and formatting, auto-appended only in recognized email clients — never in chat apps.

No tray icon, no clutter

A small status widget you can hover for quick access. Everything else stays out of your way until you need it.

Three hotkeys

Pick the mode that fits the moment.

Each one is its own hotkey, rebindable in Settings. Use whichever matches how much control you want over a given reply.

Mode A

Direct reply

Silent, no popup. Reads the screen and pastes a draft immediately using your default tone and signature.

Ctrl + J
Mode B

Add context

A quick popup to pick a tone of voice, a signature, and add extra context before Echo reads the screen and drafts.

Ctrl + O
Mode C

Insert template

Pick a saved template and fill in the blanks. No screenshot, no AI call — just your own words, fast.

Ctrl + P
