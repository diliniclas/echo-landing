2. What Echo does

Echo is a desktop application for Windows and Mac that, on a keyboard shortcut you press, reads the message currently visible on your screen (for example an email, chat, or text message) and drafts a reply in a tone of voice you configure. Echo pastes the draft into the currently focused text field. Echo never sends anything on its own. Reviewing and sending — or not sending — any drafted reply is always your decision.

To draft a reply, Echo may capture a screenshot of the active application window, or for saved templates, only the text you provide. This content is sent to our backend, which forwards it to Anthropic's Claude API to generate the draft. See our Privacy Policy for how this data is handled.
